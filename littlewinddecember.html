High power prices following little wind

Friday the 7th of December the system price was 48,65 EUR/MWh. Denmark who produce a lot of wind power had a surplus of production and exported power to neighbouring countries. Five days later the weather situation, thus the power prices were completely different.

Wednesday the 12th of December the situation changed completely and with little wind Denmark had to import power from neighbouring countries. The system price went all the way up to 61,59 EUR/MWh. This is a consequence of having renewable energy sources, first and foremost wind power, as a central part of the power system.
